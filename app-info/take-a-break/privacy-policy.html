Privacy Policy

Information Collection, Use and Sharing

The Application does not collect, store or share any personal information.

The Application stores only data relevant to its core functionality. That data does not leave your device (phone, tablet or computer) unless you explicitly share it.

The Application will never send you a notification that is not related to this functionality.
